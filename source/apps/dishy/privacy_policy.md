# Foodii Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 6, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Foodii. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Foodii mobile application ("the App").

**Key Point: We do not collect, store, or process your personal information.** The App is designed to operate entirely on your device and your personal iCloud account.

## Summary

✅ **We DO:**
- Store your recipes locally on your device
- Use iCloud to sync your data across your devices (if you enable it)
- Send images to AI services only when you use recipe generation

❌ **We DO NOT:**
- Collect your personal information
- Track your usage or behavior
- Show advertisements
- Sell your data to third parties
- Require account registration
- Store your data on our servers

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We **do not collect** any of the following:
- Name, email address, or phone number
- User account credentials (we don't have user accounts)
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Contact information
- Usage analytics or behavior data
- Payment information (handled by Apple)

## 2. Data Storage

### 2.1 Local Storage
All your app data is stored locally on your device:
- **Recipes**: Name, calories, servings, notes, creation date
- **Photos**: Food images and thumbnails
- **Ingredients**: Name, quantity, unit, order
- **Steps**: Cooking instructions
- **Tags**: Category labels and colors
- **Settings**: App preferences
- **API Keys**: Securely stored in device Keychain (if you provide your own)

**Storage Technology**: SwiftData framework (Apple's local database)

### 2.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your recipe data is synchronized to **your personal iCloud account**
- Data is encrypted during transmission and at rest
- Data is stored on Apple's iCloud servers (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
- We **do not have access** to your iCloud data
- You can disable iCloud sync anytime in Settings → iCloud → Foodii

**Important**: iCloud sync is controlled by you and governed by Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy.

### 2.3 No Cloud Backend
We **do not operate**:
- User databases
- Cloud storage services
- Server infrastructure for user data

## 3. AI Recipe Generation

### 3.1 What We Send to AI Services
When you use the AI recipe generation feature, we send to third-party AI providers:
- **Food Image**: The photo you select for analysis
- **Language Preference**: Your selected language
- **Existing Tags**: Your recipe tags (to maintain consistency)

### 3.2 What We Do NOT Send
We do **not** send:
- Your personal information
- Your device information
- Your recipe history
- Any user identifiers
- Your location

### 3.3 Third-Party AI Providers
You can choose which AI service to use:

**OpenAI (United States)**
- Service: GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini models
- Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

**Alibaba Cloud DashScope (China)**
- Service: Qwen models (通义千问)
- Privacy Policy: https://www.alibabacloud.com/help/en/dashscope/

**ByteDance Volcengine (China)**
- Service: Doubao models (豆包)
- Privacy Policy: https://www.volcengine.com/docs/

### 3.4 Your Control
- You choose when to use AI generation (not automatic)
- You can select which AI provider to use
- You can provide your own API keys
- Generated recipes are stored locally on your device

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not** share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties.

The only services that receive data are:
- **Apple iCloud**: Recipe data for optional sync (you control this)
- **AI Providers**: Food images only when you generate recipes (you choose when)

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Data Access
- All your data is stored locally on your device
- You can view all your recipes, photos, and settings within the App

### 8.2 Data Export
- You can export all your recipes as JSON files (`.dishy` format)
- Export includes: recipes, ingredients, steps, tags, and notes
- Photos are included in the export file

### 8.3 Data Deletion
To delete your data:
1. **Delete from Device**: Delete the App from your device (all local data is removed)
2. **Delete from iCloud**: Go to Settings → iCloud → Manage Storage → Foodii → Delete Documents and Data
3. **Delete Specific Recipes**: Swipe to delete individual recipes in the App

### 8.4 iCloud Sync Control
You can control iCloud sync:
- **Enable/Disable**: Settings → iCloud → Foodii
- **View Sync Status**: In-app Settings → Cloud Sync
- **Manual Sync**: Sync happens automatically when enabled

### 8.5 AI Provider Selection
You can choose which AI provider to use:
- Go to Settings → AI Settings
- Select your preferred provider (OpenAI, DashScope, Volcengine)
- Provide your own API keys (optional)

## 6. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained on your device until you delete it
- **iCloud Data**: Retained in your iCloud until you disable sync or delete it
- **AI Service Data**: We do not retain copies of images sent to AI services

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## 8. Data Protection Rights

### 8.1 Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data (all data is on your device)
- Correct your data (edit recipes in the App)
- Delete your data (delete the App and iCloud data)
- Export your data (use the export feature)

### 8.2 GDPR & CCPA Compliance
Since we don't collect personal data:
- We don't process personal data requiring GDPR/CCPA compliance
- Your data is under your direct control
- You can export data anytime
